Entertainment

As the tale of the night unfolds there will be a whole series of spectacular entertainment.

From a carousel and dodgems, to shisha, fire dancers and tarot card readers there will be something to entertain you.

Moreover, no arabian night would be complete without luxurious pampering and we will bring you hair styling from Mahogany, face painting, a henna artist, masseuse and fresh hand-made cosmetics from Lush.

In the main stage marquee we will have performances from:

  • Out of the Blue
  • Dots Funk Odyssey
  • Truly Medly Deeply
  • Belly Dancing

with the main headliner act:

  • Itchy Feet

And in the separate DJ marquee dance the night away to a variety of music and DJs including Supermarket and finishing the night with a silent disco.

On the intimate inside stage enjoy performances from some of Oxford's finest performers: The Oxford Gargoyles, The Oxford Belles, Oxford Bhangra Society, The Oxford Imps and The Oxford Singers

Finally, you can capture all the stories of your evening with a professional roaming photographers and a photo booth.
