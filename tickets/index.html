Tickets

Tickets for 'A Thousand and One Arabian Nights' are now sold out and we look forward to welcoming all of you who have tickets to the ball.

Ticket Wristbands will be available for collection in the days running up to the ball at the University Club, Mansfield Road, where the ball will be held. More information will be emailed to ticket holders in seventh week.

If you were not lucky enough to get a ticket and are interested in being added to the ticket waiting list please email marketing.oxfordragball@gmail.com with 'Waiting List' in the header and stating your name, college and how many tickets you would like and we will let you know if any tickets become available.

If you no longer can attend the ball and would like to sell you ticket you can do this amongst your friends by sending them your confirmation email to bring to ticket collection. Alternatively, please email marketing.oxfordragball@gmail.com with and we can put you in contact with someone who would like to buy your ticket.
